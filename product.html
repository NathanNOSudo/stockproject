Stock Watch

Stock-Watch is an easy to use web application for the every day user who needs to finds stock prices on the fly. Whether at their computer or on the run from their phone. Users can add stocks to their personalized watchlist and get immediate information about their watchlist, set push notifications, and more! Get started today!
